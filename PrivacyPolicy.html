1. Purpose

This Data Protection Policy outlines how TheraTalk.ai collects, stores, processes, and protects personal and sensitive information. We are committed to safeguarding the emotional, mental, and personal privacy of our users.

2. Key Principles

We adhere to the following data protection principles:

  • Lawfulness, fairness, and transparency: We process data lawfully, fairly, and transparently
  • Purpose limitation: We only use data for what it was collected for
  • Data minimization: We only collect what we need
  • Accuracy: We keep data up to date
  • Storage limitation: We retain data only as long as needed
  • Integrity and confidentiality: We protect data with strong security measures

3. What Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, device information
  • Sensitive Data: Mood logs, chat history, mental health entries
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, usage logs
  • Optional Data: Gender, age, journal entries, feedback (provided voluntarily)

4. How We Use Your Data

Your data helps us:

  • Provide personalized mental health support
  • Improve AI conversation quality and responses
  • Send relevant notifications (you can opt out anytime)
  • Monitor platform security and performance
  • With your explicit consent, support clinical insight or academic research

5. Data Storage & Retention

We implement industry-standard security measures for your data:

  • All data is encrypted at rest and in transit
  • User chat and mental health data is stored separately from personal identifiers
  • Data is anonymized for training/improvement purposes (only if you opt in)
  • We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill services or meet legal obligations

Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access a copy of your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

6. Third Parties & AI Systems

We work with trusted partners to deliver our services:

  • AI interactions are powered by [provider, e.g. OpenAI, Google Cloud]
  • We do not sell or rent your data to advertisers
  • We may use third-party analytics (e.g. Firebase, Mixpanel) strictly for service improvement

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies responsibly:

  • Essential cookies for login sessions and app functionality
  • Usage analytics to improve app performance
  • You can disable non-essential cookies anytime in settings

8. Security Measures

We implement robust security protections:

  • End-to-end encryption (TLS/SSL) for all data transfers
  • Zero-trust access control for internal systems
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing
  • Role-based permissions for internal staff
  • Comprehensive data access logging and monitoring

9. Data Breach Procedure

In the unlikely event of a data breach:

  2. We will provide full details of affected data and remediation steps
  3. We will offer support or recommendations for affected users
