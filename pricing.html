Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and plans

What are credits and how do they work?

Credits are used for interactions with our AI companions. Each conversation or activity uses credits based on complexity. With the free plan, you receive 50 credits to start and 10 credits each month. Premium plans include unlimited conversations without credit restrictions.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. If you switch from yearly to monthly, we'll prorate your remaining time. Downgrading to free will take effect at the end of your billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards through PayPal. You can also use your PayPal balance for payments. We don't store your payment details directly - all transactions are securely handled by PayPal.

Is there a free trial for Premium?

Our yearly plan includes a 7-day free trial. The monthly plan doesn't have a trial period, but you can cancel anytime within the first month for a full refund if you're not satisfied.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your Premium subscription anytime through your account settings. After cancellation, you'll retain Premium access until the end of your billing period, then revert to the Free tier.
