Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between free and premium?

Our free tier gives you limited access with 10 credits per month after your initial 50 credits. Premium provides unlimited conversations, full feature access, and all AI companions. Premium also includes priority support and advanced analytics.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or switch between monthly and yearly plans at any time. Your unused credits will carry over when you change plans.

How does billing work?

Monthly plans are billed every 30 days from your signup date. Yearly plans are billed annually and include a 7-day free trial. You can cancel anytime through your account settings.

Is there a free trial for premium?

The yearly plan includes a 7-day free trial. The monthly plan doesn't have a trial but you can cancel anytime with no long-term commitment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards through PayPal, and PayPal balance payments. All payments are processed securely through PayPal's payment system.
